Allows you to tweak damage and stagger rates on different enemy body parts.

---------------

RE2 Port of 'Enemy Hitzone Tuner' by rthomasv3

This mod allows you to tweak the damage and stagger rates on various enemy hitzones. It currently supports head, torso, arm, and leg. The game groups together different damage parts into groups, so more granular tweaks would be difficult (NOTE: RE2 is may be less stable, wont always perfectly detect each part properly but seems 80-90% accurate)

These are the sections grouped by the game, and each group seems to share damage/stagger values.

Head - just head in this group
Torso - chest and stomach
Arm - shoulder, upper arm, forearm, and hand
Leg - thigh, shin, and foot

However, each "part" of the enemy seems to have its own health bar for dismemberment.

This mod requires REFramework.
